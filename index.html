Here We Go...

July 12, 2018 by Siyan Han in Honest Blog

I'm not a good writer but I'll be an honest one. Starting a blog is probably the last thing most of my friends, even my family will think of me doing. Good thing is I have Shishuang to be my principle editor. If you catch any grammar errors, it's her fault... lol

So I've been having so many startup ideas with different hypotheses, especially in these last 2 years. I just couldn't get myself to execute my ideas. I've tried many things - attending a startup weekend, networking, trying to convince my friends to join me (like at least 10 people). I even attempted consulting for another start up in New Zealand for analytics stuff. Although none has really worked out, I think I must take a more drastic approach to force a habit of doing something small everyday. Hence I'm starting this site and blog.

This blog will document my weekly successes and failures in starting my own company. Through sharing, I'd like to inspire more people to dare to dream to make a positive impact in the world. I want show that every person is capable of doing so and I'll interview friends with different background to prove the point.

And for the site, I'd like to think of it as a platform to showcase products that I'll build with friends from different industries. From "zero to one", we'll try to build something that we can actually start a company with and see what could happen from there.

Good luck to this idea,

Han
